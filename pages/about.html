Core Experience

Six years with Epic Games — collaboratively building the sound of Fortnite.

From September 2019 to December 2026, Integral Audio Solutions delivered map audio for Fortnite Battle Royale — one of the most technically complex and commercially significant live-service games ever made. Working within Epic's production pipeline on a title with hundreds of millions of players demands a level of technical precision and creative consistency that defines how we approach every project we take on.

That tenure encompassed multiple engine transitions, the full migration to Unreal Engine 5, and the emergence of UEFN as a major creative platform. The scale, pace, and rigour of that environment is the benchmark against which we measure all of our work.

Our Approach
The most powerful audio transcends conscious perception entirely. The ear believes before the eye does, and our job is to make sure it never has a reason to doubt.
Direct Partnership

We work directly with audio directors, producers, and studio leads — no intermediaries, no account managers. You get senior-level attention from brief through to final delivery.

Technical Rigour

Audio that sounds extraordinary but breaks the pipeline is not a success. Every deliverable is tested, documented, and integrated to specification before it leaves our hands.

Production Awareness

Having shipped on live-service titles at scale, we understand the pressures of production timelines. We communicate early, flag issues clearly, and never leave a studio guessing on status.
